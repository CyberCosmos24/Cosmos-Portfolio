Drexel University Digital Development Camp

The Drexel University College of Computing & Informatics (CCI) Summer Program is an immersive experience designed for high school and college students interested in technology, coding, and cybersecurity. The program provides hands-on learning opportunities in areas such as software development, artificial intelligence, and ethical hacking.
